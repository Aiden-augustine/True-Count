Privacy Policy

TrueCount — Last updated: March 30, 2026

TrueCount ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the TrueCount iOS application ("the App"), how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use the App, we collect the following personal data:

  • Email address — provided during account registration.
  • Username — chosen by you during registration.
  • Firebase User ID (UID) — a unique identifier assigned to your account by Firebase Authentication.
  • Gameplay statistics — including hands played, win/loss records, accuracy rate, virtual bankroll balance, and session history (cards dealt, decisions made, coach feedback).

We do not collect location data, contacts, financial account information, health data, or any other sensitive personal information.

2. How We Collect Your Information

  • Firebase Authentication — handles account creation and login using your email address and password.
  • Cloud Firestore — stores your profile and gameplay data in Google's cloud database service.

All data transmission is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS.

3. Why We Collect Your Information

  • Account management — to authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • Session history — to let you review past gameplay sessions and track your progress over time.
  • Leaderboard — your username and stats may appear on in-app leaderboards visible to other users.
  • App functionality — to personalise the coaching experience and persist your settings across devices.

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it for any purpose beyond operating the App.

4. Google / Firebase Services

The App is built on Google's Firebase platform. By using the App you are also subject to Google's Privacy Policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy. Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore are the only Firebase services used; Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics are not enabled.

5. Data Retention

Your account data (email, username, gameplay statistics) is retained for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, all associated data is deleted from our Firestore database within 30 days. Session history stored locally on your device is removed when you delete the App.

6. How to Delete Your Data

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us at cuong20040607@gmail.com. Please use the subject line "Data Deletion Request — TrueCount" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm via email when complete.

7. Children's Privacy

TrueCount is rated 17+ and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under 17. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us at the email address below and we will promptly delete the account.

8. Data Security

We use Google Firebase's built-in security features, including Firestore Security Rules and encrypted data transmission. While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your data (see Section 6).
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top. For significant changes, we will provide notice within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
